How To Guide

Planner

Your live diary for schedule, bookings, and availability.

1. Open the Planner

Where to go: Use the left-hand menu and click Planner.

What you'll see:

  • A calendar view (Day / Week / Month)
  • An Upcoming Bookings panel on the left
  • Tools at the top such as Product Filter, Find Booking, and Post Schedule

2. Switch between Day / Week / Month views

Use the view selector at the top-right of the Planner to toggle between Day, Week, and Month.

  • Day: best for detailed timing and same-day management
  • Week: best for planning your week and moving bookings
  • Month: best for high-level visibility and spotting busy periods

3. Filter your calendar by product

Use Product Filter at the top of the Planner to show only the sessions/products you want to focus on.

  • Filter to reduce clutter when you run multiple session types
  • Use it when reviewing a specific programme, class, or service

4. Colour-code your Planner

Planner items can be colour-coded to make busy diaries easier to read.

You can colour-code by:

  • Staff / trainer
  • Location / venue
  • Product or session type

Tip: If you manage multiple venues or trainers, colour-coding makes conflicts and patterns visible at a glance.

5. Add breaks, buffers, and travel time

To protect your time between bookings, set buffer rules in staff settings.

  • Add buffer time before and/or after sessions
  • Use buffers to handle travel, setup, cleanup, or admin
  • Breaks and travel time can be set per staff or resource

6. Reschedule by drag and drop

You can move bookings by dragging them to a new time slot (or to a different staff member where applicable).

  • Click and hold the booking
  • Drag it to the new slot
  • Drop to confirm

What happens next:

  • The Planner automatically checks for conflicts
  • If a clash exists (staff/room/resource), you'll be warned before confirming

7. Add an ad hoc / walk-in booking

To create a walk-in booking directly from the Planner:

  1. Click the time slot you want to book
  2. Select Add Booking
  3. Choose New Client or Existing Client
  4. Enter guest details (name, email, phone) and click Add
  5. Continue to complete the booking

8. Assign bookings to different staff or resources

You can reassign bookings to another available staff member or location/resource when needed.

  • Move the booking to a new slot/staff (drag and drop)
  • Or edit the booking and change the assigned staff/resource

Tip: This is ideal for covering sickness, swapping trainers, or moving sessions between rooms.

9. Message attendees

Use Planner messaging to contact everyone booked onto an activity/session.

How it works:

  • Open the booking/activity
  • Select Message Attendees
  • Type your message (up to the character limit shown)
  • Click Send

Please note: Messages can be shared with current attendees and also automatically shown to future attendees who book later (depending on your activity messaging rules).

10. Share your activity

You can share an activity directly from the Planner to drive bookings.

Share options may include:

  • Email
  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • WhatsApp
  • X

11. Share payment links from the Planner

Need payment fast? Send a secure payment link from a booking.

  1. Open the booking in the Planner
  2. Click Send Payment Link
  3. The client receives a secure link to complete payment immediately

12. Set recurring appointments

To create repeat bookings, set recurrence when creating or editing a booking.

Recurrence options typically include:

  • Daily
  • Weekly
  • Monthly
  • Custom recurrence

13. Sync with external calendars

Keep everything aligned using external calendar integrations.

  • Connect iCal / Google / Outlook for real-time schedule sync
  • Two-way sync supported (changes in external calendars can update Fibodo)

14. Export or print your schedule

Planner schedules can be exported for reporting, payroll, or offline planning.

  • Export as PDF or CSV
  • Print directly from the Planner view

15. Notifications when bookings change

When you edit a booking, notifications can be triggered automatically.

  • Email notifications
  • Push notifications (where enabled)
  • Configurable per booking/activity type

Planner best practice

  • Use Week view for day-to-day operations
  • Use Month view for forward planning and spotting busy periods
  • Set buffers early to avoid back-to-back stress and travel clashes
  • Use messaging to reduce no-shows and keep clients informed
