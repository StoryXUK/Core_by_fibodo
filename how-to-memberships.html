Memberships

Build loyalty. Unlock recurring revenue.

Go to the Memberships section in your dashboard. Click "Create New Tier" and set: Name & description, Price & billing interval, Benefits (access to classes, content, discounts, or special events). Assign tiers to products, content, or events as needed.

Set billing frequency (weekly, monthly, annually) for each tier. Proration is available if members upgrade, downgrade, or join mid-cycle, ensuring fair billing. Fibodo calculates charges automatically and generates invoices/receipts.
