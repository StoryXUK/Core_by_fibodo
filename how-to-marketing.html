Marketing How-To Guide

The Marketing section in CORE by fibodo gives you built-in tools to promote your business, engage clients, and strengthen your brand — all without needing third-party platforms.

1. Open Marketing

Where to go:
From the left-hand menu, click Marketing.

What you’ll see:

  • Social Media Accounts
  • Email Branding
  • Push Notifications
  • Asset Branding
  • Reviews

2. Connect social media accounts

Connect your social platforms to share activity and grow your audience.

Supported platforms include:

  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • X (Twitter)
  • TikTok

Click Connect next to the platform and follow the on-screen steps.

3. Email branding

Email Branding allows you to customise automated emails sent to clients.

You can edit:

  • Booking confirmation emails
  • Reminder emails
  • Follow-up emails sent after sessions

Each template can include your branding, tone of voice, and links.

4. Push notifications

Send instant messages directly to your clients’ devices.

To create a push notification:

  1. Click Push Notifications.
  2. Select New Push Notification.
  3. Add a title and message.
  4. Choose recipients.
  5. Send immediately or schedule for later.

Push notifications are ideal for reminders, promotions, or last-minute updates.

5. Asset branding – ready-to-print posters

Asset Branding gives you professionally designed, prebuilt marketing assets.

Currently available:

  • Posters with your branding and business details

What makes this powerful:

  • Pre-designed layouts — no design skills required
  • Automatically uses your business information
  • Optimised for printing or sharing

You can preview the poster on screen or print it instantly.

6. Reviews

The Reviews section shows feedback collected from your clients.

From here you can:

  • View ratings and feedback
  • Track your overall rating
  • Monitor reputation over time

Strong reviews help build trust and improve conversions.

7. Marketing best practice

  • Connect social accounts early to maximise reach
  • Keep email templates clear and on-brand
  • Use push notifications sparingly for high-impact messages
  • Print asset branding posters for studios, gyms, or events
  • Monitor reviews regularly and respond to feedback

Result:
Marketing tools in fibodo help you promote your business, stay connected with clients, and present a professional brand — all from one simple dashboard.
