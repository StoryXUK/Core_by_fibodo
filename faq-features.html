Payments FAQs

CORE integrates with secure payment processors to allow you to accept payments online or in person. Simply connect your payment account in settings and start accepting card payments instantly.
CORE supports all major credit and debit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers. Payment options can be customized based on your business needs.

Bookings FAQs

Clients can book sessions 24/7 through your booking page, website, or the CORE app. They can view available times, select their preferred slot, and book instantly.
Yes, you can set capacity limits, booking windows, cancellation policies, and buffer times between sessions to manage your schedule effectively.
