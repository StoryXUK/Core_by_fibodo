How to Set Up Your Business Profile

Follow these steps to complete your business profile in CORE by fibodo and get ready to take bookings and payments. The steps below follow the exact order of the onboarding process.

Step 1: Complete Your Business Profile (Mandatory)

What you’ll see:
A prompt titled “Business Profile (Mandatory Step)”.

What to do:

  • Click Edit in the Business Profile section.
  • Enter your business details, including:
    • Business name
    • Tagline (optional)
    • Contact number
    • Email address
    • About you / your business
    • Website (if applicable)
  • Save your changes.

Why this matters:
Your business name is used to automatically generate your unique booking URL, which clients will use to find and book you.

Step 2: Add Company Information

What you’ll see:
A prompt titled “Company Information”.

What to do:

  • Click Edit in the Company Information panel.
  • Add the following details:
    • Registered company name
    • Business address
    • VAT number (if applicable)
  • Save your details.

Why this matters:
These details appear on receipts, invoices, and system emails, helping keep your business professional and compliant.

Step 3: Upload Profile & Cover Images

What you’ll see:
A prompt titled “Profile & Cover Images”.

What to do:

  • Click the edit icon on the profile header.
  • Upload:
    • A profile image (logo or personal photo)
    • A cover image (branding, venue, or action shot)
  • Save once uploaded.

Why this matters:
Your images appear across your booking pages and apps, giving clients confidence and reinforcing your brand.

Step 4: Select Your Booking App

What you’ll see:
A prompt titled “Select Your Booking App”.

What to do:

  • Select the booking app that best fits your business sector.
  • Confirm your selection.

Why this matters:

  • Your QR code and booking links are generated using this app.
  • These appear across your website, social media, and marketing assets.
  • Once selected, changes require contacting fibodo support.

Step 5: Review Your Booking URL & Client Code

What you’ll see:
A section titled “Please see below your unique website URL and customer App”.

What to check:

  • Your automatically generated Profile URL
  • Your unique Client Code

How to use them:

  • Embed the booking URL on your website
  • Share it on social media
  • Use the QR code in marketing materials
  • Direct clients straight to booking with no admin

Step 6: Add Your Bank Account (Getting Paid)

What you’ll see:
The Getting Paid section with an Add an account button.

What to do:

  • Click Add an account.
  • Enter your bank account details.
  • Save to activate payments.

Why this matters:
Payments from bookings can’t be released until a bank account is connected.

You’re All Set

Once these steps are complete:

  • Your booking pages are live
  • Your diary runs automatically
  • Clients can book and pay without back-and-forth
  • Everything syncs into your planner and finances
