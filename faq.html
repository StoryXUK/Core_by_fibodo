Payments

Instant, secure, and automatic — get booked and get paid without delay.

How do I get paid through fibodo Payments?

Payments are processed automatically when clients book or purchase. Funds are transferred to your linked bank account according to your payout schedule.

How do I link my bank account for payouts?

Go to Settings → Payments → Bank Details and enter your account information. Fibodo uses secure verification to confirm your account.

Are payouts instant or is there a holding period?

Standard payouts are processed within 2-3 business days. Instant payouts may be available depending on your plan and region.

Which cards and payment methods are accepted?

Fibodo accepts all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and digital wallets.

Does Fibodo support Apple Pay and Google Pay at checkout?

Yes — both Apple Pay and Google Pay are supported for faster, more secure checkout on all devices.

What are the transaction fees and do they vary by plan?

Transaction fees vary by plan. CORE Starter includes standard processing fees, while higher plans offer reduced rates. Check the pricing page for details.

How are refunds and partial refunds processed?

You can issue full or partial refunds directly from the booking or order details. Refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

Can I issue credits instead of cash refunds?

Yes — you can offer account credits or vouchers as an alternative to cash refunds, which clients can use for future bookings.

How do I set custom cancellation/refund policies per product?

When creating or editing a product, go to the Policies section and define cancellation windows, refund percentages, and no-show fees.

Can I accept payments in person via the Business App?

Yes — use the Business App to process card payments, cash, or bank transfers on-site. All transactions sync automatically with your account.

Is tipping supported at checkout or in person?

Yes — enable tipping at checkout or in the Business App. Clients can add a tip amount or percentage before completing payment.

Can I charge no-show fees automatically?

Yes — set no-show fees in your product policies. If a client doesn't attend and doesn't cancel in time, the fee is charged automatically.

How do I handle chargebacks and disputes?

Fibodo notifies you of any disputes and provides tools to submit evidence. You can respond directly through the Payments dashboard.

Can I split payments between multiple trainers or locations?

Yes — set up payment splits in Settings to automatically distribute revenue between team members or locations based on percentages or fixed amounts.

Does Fibodo support instalment payments or payment plans?

Yes — create payment plans with custom schedules. Clients can pay in instalments, and you receive funds according to your payout schedule.

How do I export payment history for accounting?

Go to Payments → Reports and export transactions as CSV or PDF. Filter by date range, payment method, or status for easy reconciliation.

Can I set different tax rates (e.g., VAT) per product or location?

Yes — configure tax rates in Settings → Taxes and apply them to specific products, services, or locations. Taxes are calculated automatically at checkout.

Are saved cards tokenised and PCI compliant?

Yes — all card data is tokenised and stored securely. Fibodo is fully PCI DSS compliant, ensuring the highest security standards.

How do I reconcile daily income with bank payouts?

The Payments dashboard shows daily income, pending payouts, and completed transfers. Match these with your bank statements for accurate reconciliation.

Can I accept international payments and in which currencies?

Yes — Fibodo supports multiple currencies including GBP, EUR, USD, and more. Currency conversion is handled automatically at competitive rates.

How are digital receipts sent and can I customise them?

Receipts are sent automatically via email after each transaction. You can customise the branding, logo, and footer text in Settings → Receipts.

Can I restrict payments to specific countries or regions?

Yes — set geographic restrictions in Settings → Payments to block or allow payments from specific countries or regions.

Does Fibodo support SCA / 3D Secure flows?

Yes — Strong Customer Authentication (SCA) and 3D Secure are fully supported to comply with European payment regulations and enhance security.

Can I set minimum/maximum order amounts?

Yes — configure minimum and maximum order values in Settings → Payments to control transaction limits for your business.

How do I handle donations or pay-what-you-want pricing?

Create a product with flexible pricing enabled. Clients can enter their own amount, with optional minimum and suggested amounts.

Can I track payments across multiple staff?

Yes — view payment reports filtered by staff member to track individual revenue, commissions, and performance.

What currencies are supported?

Fibodo supports major currencies including GBP, EUR, USD, AUD, CAD, and more. Set your default currency in Settings → Business Details.

Can I manage taxes or VAT in fibodo?

Yes — configure tax rates in Settings → Taxes. Apply different rates to products, services, or locations, and generate tax reports for compliance.
