How To Guide

Quick Setup

Set up scheduling so your diary runs itself — no double bookings, no message chasing, no admin stress.

Step 1: Create or Select a Product

  1. Go to your Product Library.
  2. Create a new product or select an existing one.
  3. Choose the product type:
    • 1:1 session
    • Class
    • Course or programme

Each product in fibodo has its own schedule, which is what makes automated booking possible.

Step 2: Set the Product Schedule

For each product, define how and when it can be booked.

  1. Set the duration (e.g. 30, 45, 60 minutes).
  2. Add any buffer time before or after sessions if needed.
  3. Choose whether it's:
    • One-off
    • Recurring (e.g. weekly class)
    • Multi-session programme
  4. If it's a class, set the capacity (number of spaces available).

This tells fibodo exactly how the product fits into your diary.

Step 3: Set Your Availability

Next, tell fibodo when you're available to work.

  1. Open Availability Settings.
  2. Add your working days and times.
  3. Assign locations if relevant:
    • In-person
    • Online
    • Or both
  4. Apply availability to:
    • One product
    • Or multiple products at once

fibodo now understands when you can work and what you're offering during that time.

Step 4: Connect Products and Availability

This is where the automation happens.

fibodo automatically connects:

  • What you sell (your products)
  • When you're available
  • Who's booking

Clients will only see time slots that genuinely work for you based on:

  • Your availability
  • The product's duration and rules
  • Existing bookings in your planner

No manual checking required.

Step 5: Go Live with Booking

Once your product and availability are set:

  1. Share your website link or app QR code.
  2. Clients can now book instantly, 24/7.

When someone books:

  • The time slot is instantly locked
  • The booking is confirmed
  • Your planner updates automatically in real time

No clashes. No follow-up messages. No manual diary updates.

What This Means in Practice

  • You can schedule multiple products at the same time
  • Your diary stays accurate automatically
  • Clients always book the right session, at the right time
  • You spend less time managing bookings — and more time with clients

You're Done

Once your products and availability are set, fibodo keeps everything in sync in the background — so your scheduling just works.
