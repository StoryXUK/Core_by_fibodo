How to Build a Product

This guide walks you through setting up a product such as a class, session, or event. Follow each section in order to make sure customers have all the information they need to book with confidence.

1. Overview

Use the Overview section to introduce your class or session.

  • Add a short subtitle.
  • Write a clear description explaining what the session involves, who it’s for, and what customers can expect.

A simple, honest description helps customers understand the value of the session and increases bookings.

2. Pricing

Set up pricing and availability for your session.

  • Enter the total number of spaces available for this session. This helps customers see how many spots are open and encourages them to book early.
  • Set the full price for the session, or mark it as free if there is no charge. Clear pricing makes booking simple and avoids confusion.
  • Choose the refund policy that applies to this session.
  • The options in this list come from your admin settings. Refund policies can only be edited or updated in the admin section.

3. Location

Use this space to tell customers exactly how to find you.

Include helpful details such as:

  • Where the entrance is
  • How long it takes to walk from the nearest station
  • Closest public transport links
  • Whether parking is available
  • How early customers should arrive

Clear directions remove confusion and ensure sessions start smoothly.

Enter your full physical address and choose the correct location from the suggestions.

If the map pin isn’t in the right place, simply drag it to the exact spot — this ensures customers arrive at the right location every time.

Accurate location details help customers find you quickly and avoid missed sessions.

4. Scheduling

Choose the days and times your session will run each week.

  • You can add multiple time slots per day if needed.
  • Use the alternative schedule option if you run different hours on certain weeks or seasons — perfect for holidays, term-time adjustments, or rotating timetables.

A clear, consistent schedule ensures customers always know when they can book.
