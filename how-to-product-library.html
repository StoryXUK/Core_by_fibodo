How to Build a Product in Fibodo

This guide walks you through creating, configuring, and managing products in Fibodo — from 1:1 sessions to classes, courses, events, and bundles.

Step 1: Create a New Product

  1. Log in to your dashboard
  2. Go to Product Library
  3. Click Add New Product
  4. Choose your product type:
    • 1:1 session
    • Class
    • Course
    • Event

Each product type automatically adapts the setup options to match how it’s delivered and booked.

Step 2: Add Core Product Details

In the product editor, configure the essentials:

  • Product name – clear and client-friendly
  • Description – explain what the client gets
  • Capacity – one-to-one or group size
  • Schedule – when the product is available
  • Pricing – base price or tiered pricing

You can save at any time and return later.

Step 3: Add Rich Content (Images, Video & Files)

Enhance your product listing by adding:

  • Images
  • Videos (uploaded or embedded)
  • PDFs and downloadable resources
  • Formatted text (headings, bullet points, links)

This content appears on your website, app, and booking widgets automatically.

Step 4: Tag and Categorise for Discovery

To help clients find the right product:

  • Add tags (e.g. strength, yoga, beginners)
  • Assign categories
  • Include relevant keywords

These improve search and filtering across your site and app.

Step 5: Configure Pricing Options

Fibodo supports flexible pricing per product, including:

  • Standard pricing
  • Premium tiers
  • Promotional pricing
  • Discounts and dynamic pricing rules

Pricing rules can apply automatically without manual updates.

Step 6: Control Who Can Book the Product

You can restrict products by:

  • Location or venue
  • Staff member or team
  • Membership level

This is ideal for multi-site businesses, exclusive sessions, or staff-specific services.

Step 7: Set Availability & Scheduling Rules

Define exactly when a product can be booked:

  • Start and end dates
  • Recurring availability (days and times)
  • Blackout periods (holidays, maintenance, closures)

Only valid time slots are shown to clients — no clashes, no admin.

Step 8: Add Course Prerequisites (If Needed)

For courses or structured programmes, you can require:

  • Completion of previous courses
  • Attendance at specific sessions
  • Required skills or experience

Clients are guided automatically if prerequisites aren’t met.

Step 9: Bundle Products into Packages

Create bundles such as:

  • Multi-session packs
  • Course bundles
  • Event combinations

Bundles can include built-in discounts or special pricing.

Step 10: Set Visibility & Privacy Options

Control how and where products appear:

  • Public – visible on website and app
  • Hidden – available via direct link only
  • Private/Internal – admin or staff use only

Perfect for testing, pilots, or internal services.

Step 11: Duplicate Existing Products

To save time:

  1. Select an existing product
  2. Click Duplicate

All settings are copied, including:

  • Schedules
  • Pricing
  • Add-ons
  • Resources

Ideal for repeating formats or seasonal variations.

Step 12: Sync Automatically Across All Channels

Any change you make is instantly synced to:

  • Your public website
  • Mobile apps
  • Booking widgets

No republishing, no manual updates.

Step 13: Archive or Retire Old Products

When a product is no longer available:

  • Mark it as Archived or Inactive

Archived products:

  • Stay in the system for reporting
  • Are removed from public booking

Final Tip

Think of products as living assets. You can refine pricing, availability, content, and access at any time — without disrupting your clients or your schedule.
